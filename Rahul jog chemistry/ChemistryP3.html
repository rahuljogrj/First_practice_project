Chromatography procedure activity:-

 Leave the paper in the solvent until the solvent has nearly reached the top of the paper.

 Allow the paper to dry. then measure the distance between your original pencil line and the line that
   the solvent reached.

 Remove the paper from the solvent, and use a pencil to mark where the solvent has reached.

 Fasten the paper to a pencil, splint or glass rod.

 Draw a pencil line about 2 cm from the bottom of the paper.

 Draw a sample of each coloured ink on the pencil line and let it dry.

 Put about a 1 cm depth of solvent into the beaker.

 Label the samples on the paper using a pencil.

 Put the paper into the beaker so that the end of the end of it just dips into the solvent.
